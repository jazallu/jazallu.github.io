How GIRL Works

Finding your new circle of friends is simple and intuitive with GIRL

Select Your College

Sign up and select your college or university. This helps us connect you with other students at your school looking for friends or roommates.

1

Create Your Profile

Create your profile showcasing your personality, interests, and what you're looking for in friends or roommates. Add photos that express who you are.

2

Discover Matches

Browse through potential friends and roommates at your college. Our algorithm suggests matches based on compatibility and shared interests.

3

Swipe & Match

Swipe right on profiles you like. When someone you've swiped right on also swipes right on your profile, it's a match! Now you can start chatting.

4

Connect & Meet Up

Chat with your matches through our secure messaging system. When you feel comfortable, arrange to meet up on campus or a nearby spot to begin your friendship.

5

Meet the Founder

Jasmitha Allu

Jasmitha is a driven high school student from Minneapolis, Minnesota with a passion for technology, cybersecurity, and creating innovative solutions. As a member of Business Professionals of America and a varsity tennis athlete, she brings dedication and passion to everything she does.

Building GIRL from the ground up, Jasmitha designed the user experience, developed key features, and ensured everything runs smoothly with Firebase and Apple Developer tools. Her vision is to create a safe space for girls to connect, find friends, and build meaningful relationships.

Jasmitha is an award-winning young technologist, recognized in the Minnesota Aspirations in Computing Awards. She plans to pursue a degree in Cybersecurity with a minor in Business.
